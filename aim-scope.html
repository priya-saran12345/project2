AIM AND SCOPE

Aim



International Journal of Technical Research & Science (Published by: Institute of Technical and Scientific Research) is having ISSN 2454-2024 (online), is open access peer reviewed & refereed journal. The journal is published monthly issue in English. This international multidisciplinary research journal provided an intellectual platform for the scholars, research students and academicians to share knowledge in the form innovative articles in all field .This journal aims to promote multidisciplinary knowledge and values for the development of society and extension of human knowledge.



SCOPE

The IJTRS is devoted to publishing innovative thoughts and research paper on variety of topics related to engineering, science, arts, humanities, social science, education, physical education, business, law, communication, biological science, computer application, Information technology, library science, literature, anthropology, economics, communication studies, corporate governance, criminology, cross-cultural studies, psychology, demography, development studies, ethics, industrial relations, information science, international relations, linguistics, media studies, visual arts, methodology, philosophy, political science, geography, history, population Studies, public administration, sociology, social welfare, performing arts (music, theatre & dance), religious studies, women studies etc. The journal is published in online version only. IJTRS publishes original papers, review papers, analytical and simulation models, case studies, empirical research, book reviews, conceptual technical notes, and framework. Special Issues fully devoted to important topics or issues will also be published..
